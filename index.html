Since the 1990s, the Mercedes-Benz Classic Center in Fellbach has uniquely combined the know-how and
services of Mercedes-Benz Classic in all aspects of classic vehicles bearing the three-pointed star.
It offers a comprehensive range of services.

Services from the manufacturer itself.

The workshop with its technical experts specialises in work ranging from repairs and inspections to factory restorations to the highest standards. This also includes close cooperation with the archives of Mercedes-Benz, for example for access to original vehicle documents.
The Classic Center and Archives also work together on expert reports documenting the condition and originality of a vehicle. One of the Classic Center’s internationally sought-after services is supplying customers with genuine replacement parts for classic Mercedes-Benz vehicles. The Center also sells exclusive classics: one of these vehicles could become your own dream car.

Genuine Spare Parts.

Spare parts from Mercedes-Benz Classic Spare parts from Mercedes-Benz Classic stand for the highest quality down to the last detail. This service of the Classic Center contributes to the long-term preservation of the brand’s classic cars in authentic condition. By regularly producing new parts exactly to manufacturer specifications, Mercedes-Benz Classic offers a comprehensive parts range. When reproducing replacement parts, the Classic Center also draws on the know-how of the Archives. In this way, each part contributes to the perfect whole: a classic dream car from Mercedes-Benz.

Classic Workshop.

From factory restoration with strict attention to originality according to the manufacturer’s standards to inspection and maintenance for reliable operation: the workshop of the Mercedes-Benz Classic Center offers owners of high-quality classic cars bearing the famous star an all-round service at the highest level. Factory-trained specialists with comprehensive know-how work as if in a manufactory. Services also include repairs, including body, engine and electrics. The support provided by Mercedes-Benz Classic – right down to the Classic Center stamp in the service book – ensures the authenticity of the vehicle and helps to preserve its value. Since the foundation of the Mercedes-Benz Classic Center, the Mercedes-Benz Classic Collection and the Mercedes-Benz Museum have also relied on the workshop’s skills and knowledge.

Manufacturer’s expert assessment.

Experts from the Classic Center and the Mercedes-Benz Classic Archives work together to produce individual vehicle appraisals. These documents provide detailed evidence of the status and history of high-quality Mercedes-Benz classics, and vehicles by the preceding companies. Each appraisal includes extensive archive research as well as examination of the vehicle itself by the workshop specialists. This scrutiny includes authentic materials, matching vehicle numbers and original part specifications, for example. Expert appraisals by Mercedes-Benz Classic are recognised worldwide as documents with unique significance.
